# Product Development

Master product strategy, user-centered design, and agile development processes to build products that users love.

## Core Areas

- **Product Strategy**: Vision, roadmapping, and market analysis
- **User Research**: User interviews, usability testing, and data analysis
- **Agile Methodologies**: Scrum, Kanban, and iterative development
- **Design Thinking**: Prototyping, user experience, and design systems
- **Metrics & Analytics**: KPIs, A/B testing, and product analytics
- **Stakeholder Management**: Communication, alignment, and delivery
